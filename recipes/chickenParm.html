Chicken Parm

Description

A breaded chicken breast with tomato sauce and cheese.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Breading

Steps

  1. Cover chicken in breading
  2. Cover breaded chicken breast in tomato paste
  3. Sprinkle chicken breast with cheese
  4. Bake in oven
  5. Enjoy!
